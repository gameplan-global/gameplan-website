Many people are involved in a new business launch, so you need one plan that everyone and works from.

With GamePlan, everyone understands the plan because it\'s visual. People stay focused on completing their tasks because they know their impact on the launch.

Benefits of planning

Planning makes you consider all aspects of the business launch. This is critical when so much is at stake.

Planning shows the project\'s critical path (with red arrows). These are the tasks that will impact the business start up date if they run late.

As you visually plan, GamePlan creates a resource-leveled project schedule. The schedule is achievable because each person\'s availability is considered and no one is double-booked.

The project schedule is shown in a Gantt chart immediately below the Whiteboard.

Benefits of Scheduling

The schedule shows you the earliest possible new business launch date given the work required and each person\'s availability.

The schedule shows everyone exactly when they need to start and finish their tasks in order to keep the launch on time.

Tracking is done once the project is underway by setting tasks as completed or entering timesheets. Tracking keeps the project plan up to date.

Benefits of Tracking

Tracking keeps the schedule valid, so that everyone can rely on it. When there\'s a delay or when a task takes longer than expected, the downstream schedule updates.

Tracking the project also shows the project status. How far through the project are you? When will you be ready to launch? This information and more is always known.
