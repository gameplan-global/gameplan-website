As the saying goes: "If you don\'t plan, you are planning to fail."

This is especially true for event planning when the event date cannot slide if you\'re not ready.

Yet many Event Planners don\'t plan with the right tools because planning tools have, until now, been hard to use. GamePlan changes that. Anyone can plan in GamePlan. It\'s easy, it\'s fast, and it\'s visual.

Benefits of planning

Planning your event makes you consider the details ahead of time, so there are fewer surprises as you get closer to the big day.

Planning also shows you if you have the resources that you need to be ready in time.

Once you have a plan, all you need to do is follow it and you\'ll be fully prepared for your event. With much less stress.

GamePlan creates a resource-leveled project schedule from the project plan. A resource-leveled schedule is an achievable schedule because people are not double-booked.

When any change is made to the plan, the schedule is automatically updated.

Benefits of Scheduling

The schedule tells you when all of your project deliverable dates will be achieved, so you can communicate these dates with confidence.

The schedule also shows your subcontractors when their tasks will be ready to work on. This eliminates wasted time and keeps the project on track.

Some builders don\'t plan because they believe that the plan quickly gets out of date. This is true if you do not track your project. When you track your project, your plan stays up to date.

Benefits of Tracking

Tracking keeps your schedule valid, so that everyone can rely on the schedule. When there\'s a delay or when a task takes longer than expected, the downstream schedule updates.

Tracking the project also shows the project status. How far through the build are you? When will it finish? This information and more is always known.
